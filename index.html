How We Help

From capturing data to delivering production-ready outcomes

This section should explain the wider business model without forcing the user to understand every individual service immediately. It needs to make the end-to-end support clear.

Capture

Handheld laser scanning, portable laser scanning, CMM laser scanning, touch probe CMM and CT scanning.

Design

Reverse engineering from straightforward parts through to highly complex geometry, including legacy, worn and difficult-to-access components.

Validate

Inspection, GD&T, full ISIR support, colour maps and high-end dimensional reporting where traceability and accuracy matter.

Manufacture

3D printing, CNC machining, DFM support, sourcing of external processes and guidance toward the right production route.

Use Cases

Common problems we help solve

This section is important for conversion. Visitors should be able to recognise their own problem quickly rather than having to decode service names or technical terms.

No CAD available

Reverse engineer existing parts into usable models for redesign, manufacture or documentation.

Worn or damaged parts

Assess design intent and recreate parts for remanufacture, repair or further engineering review.

Need high-end inspection

Support advanced reporting requirements including GD&T, full ISIRs and complex validation work.

Internal cavities cannot be destroyed

Use CT or alternative replica methods when destructive access is not suitable.

Need a route to manufacture

Support prototypes and production decisions through 3D printing, CNC machining and sourcing of specialist processes.

Large or specialist structures

Capture, process and work with larger structures, assemblies and project-specific geometry challenges.

Sector Positioning

We work across a wide range of sectors by focusing on the engineering challenge

Positioning note

The website should not claim deep specialism in every sector. Instead, it should position the business as adaptable, technically capable and outcome-focused, working closely with each client to understand the requirement and deliver the right route forward.

This is stronger and more honest than trying to look like a niche sector specialist.

Example sector references

Automotive Infrastructure Manufacturing Energy Industrial Equipment Specialist One-Off Projects

Use real project examples and case studies to show sector range, rather than weak generic industry pages with little substance.
