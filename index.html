How we help

Show the engineering journey, not just the tools

The core site should make the broader business easy to understand. This section explains the bigger offer without forcing visitors to understand every detailed service immediately.

Capture

Handheld scanning, portable scanning, CMM laser scanning, touch probe CMM and CT scanning depending on the part, project and accuracy required.

Recreate

Reverse engineering of simple and highly complex parts, including design intent, legacy parts and difficult geometry.

Validate

Inspection work from standard comparisons through to advanced GD&T and full ISIR requirements.

Support manufacture

3D printing, CNC machining, DFM support and access to specialist suppliers and supporting partners.

Sector positioning

Show sector range without pretending to be specialists in everything

Positioning approach

The business works across a wide range of sectors. The site should not over-claim expertise in every one of them. A stronger and more honest position is that Laser Scanning understands engineering challenges and adapts the route around the required outcome.

Automotive Infrastructure Manufacturing Energy Industrial Equipment

How to show this

  • Use real examples rather than generic claims
  • Let case studies prove credibility
  • Keep problem-solving language stronger than sector language

Phase 2: organic landing pages

After the core site is fixed, build targeted pages around specific engineering problems

This is the second phase of the website strategy. The aim is not to make the company appear to be specialists in every sector. The aim is to build highly relevant pages around real engineering problems and high-intent searches so the business attracts better organic enquiries.

Reverse engineering without CAD
